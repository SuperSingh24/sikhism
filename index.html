SIKHISM AND THE PUNJABI CULTURE





















Sikhism is the world's fifth-largest religion, which was founded in the Punjab region of India about 500 years ago. There are about 25 million Sikhs around the world, of which most of them live in India, however, about, 500,000 make U.S their home. Sikhism is a diverse religion, with people from all corners of the earth
